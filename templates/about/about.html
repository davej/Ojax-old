The Ojax++ Virtual Research Environment uses Web 2.0 technologies such as Ajax, Web Services and collaborative tagging to integrate a sophisticated recommendation engine combining AI technologies and ideas from case-based reasoning (CBR), user profiling and data mining research. Ojax is a component of Ojax++.

JISC defines a VRE as:
“A set of online tools and other network resources and technologies interoperating with each other to support or enhance the processes of a wide range of research practitioners within and across disciplinary and institutional boundaries…”
